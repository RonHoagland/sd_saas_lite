# Part 1 -- Executive Summary

ServiceTitan is a cloud-based software platform designed specifically
for service contractors such as HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and similar
trades. It combines CRM, dispatching, scheduling, job management,
invoicing, payments, marketing automation, and analytics into a single
integrated platform.

The company was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in Glendale,
California. It has grown into one of the dominant vertical SaaS
platforms for the trades industry. The platform helps contractors manage
operations, increase efficiency, and scale their businesses.

# Part 21 -- Strategic Lessons

Vertical SaaS dominates specialized industries.

Understanding the workflow of the customer is more important than
feature count.

Operational data becomes a long-term competitive moat.

Switching costs create powerful customer lock-in.
