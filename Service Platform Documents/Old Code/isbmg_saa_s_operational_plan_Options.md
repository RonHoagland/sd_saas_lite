# 2. Product Tier Structure

## Lite – $24 per seat
- Max 10 seats
- 3GB storage
- Manual workflow

- Automation

## Pro – $98 per seat

- Advanced reporting

## Enterprise – $145 per seat (Future Tier)
- Enhanced scaling tools
- Extended automation rules
- Advanced reporting dashboards
- Priority support

# 16. Ownership & Profit Distribution Model

## Ownership Structure
- Founder 1: 70%
- Founder 2: 30%

Assumptions:
- Profits distributed quarterly after operating reserves maintained.
- Minimum 3–6 months operating expense retained before distributions.

## Profit Distribution Formula

Net Profit = MRR – Operating Expenses – Taxes – Reserve Allocation

Founder 1 Distribution = 70% of Net Profit  
Founder 2 Distribution = 30% of Net Profit

Example at $3,000 MRR:
- Operating Expenses ≈ $800/month
- Net Before Tax ≈ $2,200

Founder 1 ≈ $1,540/month  
Founder 2 ≈ $660/month

Example at $6,000 MRR:
- Operating Expenses ≈ $1,200/month (scaled infra)
- Net Before Tax ≈ $4,800

Founder 1 ≈ $3,360/month  
Founder 2 ≈ $1,440/month

# 18. Sensitivity Analysis

## Churn Impact (Monthly)

If churn = 2% monthly:
- Stable compounding growth

If churn = 5% monthly:
- Growth slows significantly
- Requires 2–3 new customers per month just to offset losses

If churn = 8%+ monthly:
- Unsustainable
- Must fix product/market fit immediately

---

## Seat Expansion Impact

If average seats increase from 3 → 4:
- 33% revenue increase per company
- Faster path to $6K MRR

Seat expansion is more powerful than new customer acquisition.
